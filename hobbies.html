Favorites of 2023 - Books

This was a good year for reading. Every book I committed to ended up being somewhere between good and great. That said, Michael J. Sullivan's "Legends of the First Empire" series was definitely my favorite read of 2023.

Favorites of 2023 - Games

I have a harder time getting into games than I do books, but 2023 was a strong year on the gaming front as well. The Game of the Year Awards will have a lot of great titles to choose from this year, but for me personally, Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom was definitely my most memorable gaming project. I suspect Baldur's Gate 3 might end up de-throning Tears of the Kingdom, but I haven't gotten far enough into that one to say yet.

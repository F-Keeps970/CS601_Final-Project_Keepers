Fred Keepers is a level two technical support engineer currently employed at DAWSON, a Department of Defense contractor. Although Fred works in IT, this is a fairly recent development. His first career was in special education, where he worked primarily with students on the autism spectrum. As an educator, Fred enjoyed designing specialized curriculum, working with students who displayed severe behaviors, and writing behavior intervention plans. As an IT professional, Fred enjoys solving problems with scripting and automation, collaborating with other professionals to improve processes, and writing documentation.

Outside of work, Fred lives with his partner Aidan and their two dogs in San Antonio, Texas. Aidan is a 9th grade English teacher with an interest in advanced academics. Aidan is also currently working on his National Board Certification, an advanced certification that upon completion will identify Aidan as one of the best in the profession. Fred and Aidan both enjoy a handful of hobbies including, reading, going to concerts, playing music, and playing video games. Fred is primarily a PC gamer, whereas Aidan gravitates mostly towards his PS5. Though he has recently become enamored with playing retro games on his Steam Deck
